Puster

Breathing rate indicator for infants

About

Puster helps parents and caregivers observe their infant's breathing rate using the iPhone microphone or manual tap counting. It provides an age-appropriate indication of breathing rate in breaths per minute.

Privacy

Puster processes all audio data on-device. No audio recordings are stored or transmitted. The app does not collect personal data. If you choose to use the optional AI analysis feature, measurement data (not audio) is sent to the AI provider you configure.

Puster provides an indication of breathing rate only. It does not replace medical advice or professional monitoring.
